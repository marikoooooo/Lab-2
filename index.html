What I've been reading

I have been reading Tomb of Tut Ankh Amen vol.1 by Howard Carter lately. It is a book about the discovery of the "Boy-king" pharaoh Tutankhamun.

I picked up this book because I was interested in the process of how Tutankhamun's tomb was found since I had watched a couple of YouTube videos on it.

It is a very interesting book to me because I love ancient history, especially around the time of the Bronze Age.
